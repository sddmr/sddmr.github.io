Projects

Alsancak (USV)

"Alsancak" is a four-engine, AI-assisted, fully autonomous unmanned surface vehicle (USV) capable of executing navigation, obstacle avoidance, and path-following missions with precision. Its decision-making system is powered by onboard sensors and computer vision technologies. The USV has been designed to operate in synchronization with our custom UAV, enabling real-time coordination between aerial and marine platforms, which significantly enhances mission effectiveness. To support communication and telemetry exchange, we developed a custom ground interface called "AquaBase", allowing reliable two-way data transmission. All hardware and software components were designed and developed in-house by our team.

Python C ROS2 OpenCV Linux

Şafak (UAV)

"Şafak" is a four-rotor, AI-assisted, fully autonomous unmanned aerial vehicle (UAV) capable of performing object detection, tracking, and navigation missions with high precision. Its decision-making system relies on onboard sensors, computer vision, and real-time telemetry data. The UAV is specifically designed to operate in coordination with our custom USV, enabling synchronized aerial and marine operations that significantly improve mission effectiveness. Communication and telemetry are managed through our custom ground control interface, "AquaBase", ensuring reliable two-way data exchange. All hardware and software systems were developed entirely in-house by our team.

Python C ROS2 OpenCV Linux
